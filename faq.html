Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our resume rebuilding process, service details, and what you can expect.

General Questions

What does HireSpark do?
HireSpark rebuilds resumes from scratch for professionals who want to be taken seriously in competitive hiring markets. We focus on positioning, structure, clarity, and seniority rather than simple edits or formatting.
Who is this service for?
This service is designed for professionals, specialists, and leaders who are not getting the response they expect from their applications, despite strong experience.
What is the difference between a resume review and a full rebuild?
The resume review is a short diagnostic assessment that highlights what is holding a resume back and whether a rebuild is necessary. A full rebuild is a complete rewrite and restructuring of the resume from the ground up.
What does “resume rebuild” mean?
A resume rebuild means your resume is rewritten entirely. Content, structure, bullet points, hierarchy, and positioning are reworked to reflect your true level, impact, and scope of responsibility.
What is the turnaround time?
Delivery is typically within 48–72 hours after all required information is submitted.
How does the process work?
After purchase, clients complete a short form and upload their current resume. Our team then rebuilds the resume and delivers the final version by email.
What files do I receive?
Clients receive a clean, professional PDF resume. In some cases, an editable version may also be included for minor updates.
Are the resumes ATS-compatible?
Yes. All resumes are built with clear structure and formatting to ensure they are easy to scan by both recruiters and applicant tracking systems.
Do you offer revisions?
Yes. Clients can request revisions if something needs adjustment or clarification. We are flexible and collaborative throughout the process.
Do you tailor resumes to specific job postings?
Our rebuilds are designed to position you broadly for your target level and roles. Job-specific tailoring can be discussed after delivery if needed.
How is my data handled?
All documents and personal information are treated confidentially and used only to complete your resume.
Do you offer refunds?
Due to the custom nature of the service, refunds are generally not offered once work has started. If there is an issue, we aim to resolve it through revisions.
How can I contact support?
You can contact us by email, and we usually respond quickly.

Pricing

Is there a free option?
Yes. We offer a free resume review that gives high-level feedback and helps determine whether a rebuild is needed.
How much does a full resume rebuild cost?
Pricing is clearly listed on the site as a one-time payment.
What is included in the rebuild?
A complete rewrite, restructuring, and positioning of the resume, with a focus on clarity, impact, and seniority.
Are there optional add-ons?
Optional services such as priority delivery or assessments may be available depending on demand.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery is within 48–72 hours.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept card payments and Apple Pay through a secure checkout.
Is this a subscription?
No. All services are one-time payments.
What happens after purchase?
You will be guided to a short form to submit your information. Once completed, the rebuild process begins.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our process and results.

Do you guarantee that I will get a job or an interview?
We don’t guarantee jobs or offers. Hiring decisions are always up to the employer, and results depend on factors like how often someone applies, the roles they target, and the market they’re in.

What we do guarantee is high-quality, human-written work that clearly positions a client’s experience and strengths. In practice, many clients who were getting little to no callbacks before usually start seeing responses within weeks after a rebuild. That’s a pattern we see often, but timelines and outcomes can vary.
Is this an AI tool or an automated builder?
No. HireSpark is a human-led resume writing service, not an automated tool. Every resume is reviewed and rewritten manually based on experience, positioning, and real hiring patterns.
How long does it take to get my resume?
Typical turnaround for paid services is 48–72 hours after form submission, depending on volume.
What if I am not happy with the result?
If a client is not 100% happy with the final result, they can contact support to request tweaks or adjustments so the resume fully reflects their experience and goals.
Is my personal data safe?
Yes. All resumes and personal information are handled securely and are not shared with third parties. Submitted files may be deleted automatically after a set period for privacy.
Who is this service NOT for?
This service may not be a fit if you’re looking for guaranteed outcomes, instant results, or a fully automated tool.
What do I need to provide?
Final results depend on the accuracy and completeness of the information provided by the client. Clients are responsible for submitting correct and up-to-date details during the intake process.

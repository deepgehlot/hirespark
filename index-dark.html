Frequently Asked Questions

What does HireSpark do?
HireSpark rebuilds resumes from scratch for professionals who want to be taken seriously in competitive hiring markets. We focus on positioning, structure, clarity, and seniority rather than simple edits or formatting.
Who is this service for?
This service is designed for professionals, specialists, and leaders who are not getting the response they expect from their applications, despite strong experience.
What is the difference between a resume review and a full rebuild?
The resume review is a short diagnostic assessment that highlights what is holding a resume back and whether a rebuild is necessary. A full rebuild is a complete rewrite and restructuring of the resume from the ground up.
What does “resume rebuild” mean?
A resume rebuild means your resume is rewritten entirely. Content, structure, bullet points, hierarchy, and positioning are reworked to reflect your true level, impact, and scope of responsibility.
What is the turnaround time?
Delivery is typically within 48–72 hours after all required information is submitted.
How does the process work?
After purchase, clients complete a short form and upload their current resume. Our team then rebuilds the resume and delivers the final version by email.
What files do I receive?
Clients receive a clean, professional PDF resume. In some cases, an editable version may also be included for minor updates.
How can I contact support?
You can contact us by email, and we usually respond quickly.

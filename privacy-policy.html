Privacy Policy

Last updated: 08.01.2026

HireSpark respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name, email address, and contact details
  • Resume files and career information you voluntarily submit
  • Payment confirmation details (processed securely by third-party payment providers)
  • Basic website analytics such as page visits and device type

We only collect information that is necessary to provide our services.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Deliver resume reviews, assessments, and rebuild services
  • Communicate with you about your order or support requests
  • Improve our services and content
  • Comply with legal and accounting requirements

We do not sell your data or use it for advertising purposes.

Resume Data and Confidentiality

All resumes and personal documents are treated as confidential. Access is limited to the HireSpark team involved in delivering your service.

We will never share your resume or personal data without your explicit consent.

Payments

Payments are handled by secure third-party providers. HireSpark does not store full payment card details.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent for data usage

To make a request, contact us at: teamhirespark@gmail.com
